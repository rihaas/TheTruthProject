Asmaul Husna - The 99 Beautiful Names of Allah

"And to Allah belong the best names, so invoke Him by them." (Quran 7:180)
Explore the divine attributes and characteristics of Allah (SWT) through His 99 Beautiful Names, each representing a unique aspect of His perfection and power.

1.

ٱللَّه

Allah

**The God**. This is the proper and most comprehensive name for the Creator, encompassing all divine attributes. It signifies the one and only God, unique in His essence, attributes, and actions, to whom all worship is due.

2.

ٱلرَّحْمَـٰن

Ar-Rahman

**The Most Merciful / The Beneficent**. This name signifies Allah's expansive and universal mercy that extends to all of His creation, including both believers and disbelievers, in this worldly life. It is an attribute of His very essence.

3.

ٱلرَّحِيم

Ar-Rahim

**The Especially Merciful / The Compassionate**. This name denotes Allah's specific and enduring mercy reserved for the believers, particularly in the Hereafter. It highlights the bountiful rewards and forgiveness granted to those who truly submit to Him.

4.

ٱلْمَلِك

Al-Malik

**The Absolute Sovereign / The King**. Allah is the sole and ultimate King, possessing all dominion and authority over everything in existence. He manages all affairs without needing assistance, and His command is absolute.

5.

ٱلْقُدُّوس

Al-Quddus

**The Most Holy / The Pure One**. This name emphasizes Allah's absolute purity, perfection, and transcendence from all imperfections, faults, and human flaws. He is far removed from any deficiency.

6.

ٱلسَّلَام

As-Salam

**The Giver of Peace / The Source of Peace**. Allah is the embodiment of peace and the ultimate source of all peace and security in the universe. He grants safety and well-being to His creation.

7.

ٱلْمُؤْمِن

Al-Mu'min

**The Giver of Faith / The Granter of Security**. Allah is the one who grants faith and security to His servants. He affirms the truth of His words and promises, ensuring certainty and trust.

8.

ٱلْمُهَيْمِن

Al-Muhaymin

**The Overseer / The Protector / The Dominant One**. Allah is the ultimate Guardian and Overseer of all creation. He watches over everything, preserving and maintaining it, and His control is absolute.

9.

ٱلْعَزِيز

Al-Aziz

**The All-Mighty / The Irresistible**. Allah possesses absolute power and strength, being victorious over everything. Nothing can overcome Him, and His will is always executed.

10.

ٱلْجَبَّار

Al-Jabbar

**The Compeller / The Restorer / The Repairer**. Allah is the One who compels all to His will and is able to mend and restore anything that is broken. He has the power to enforce His decrees.

11.

ٱلْمُتَكَبِّر

Al-Mutakabbir

**The Supreme / The Majestic / The Possessor of All Greatness**. This name asserts Allah's absolute greatness and superiority over all creation. He is exalted above all things and possesses ultimate pride and grandeur.

12.

ٱلْخَالِق

Al-Khaliq

**The Creator / The Maker**. Allah is the one who creates everything from nothing, bringing things into existence according to His plan and design. He is the originator of all existence.

13.

ٱلْبَارِئ

Al-Bari'

**The Evolver / The Maker / The Fashioner**. Allah is the one who fashions and creates distinct beings from pre-existing matter, giving them specific forms and functions in perfection.

14.

ٱلْمُصَوِّر

Al-Musawwir

**The Fashioner of Forms / The Designer**. Allah is the ultimate artist and designer, who gives the most beautiful and distinct forms, colors, and shapes to His creation with precision and beauty.

15.

ٱلْغَفَّار

Al-Ghaffar

**The All-Forgiving / The Forgiver**. Allah is the one who repeatedly and abundantly forgives sins and faults, even major ones, for those who sincerely repent. He covers and conceals His servants' sins.

16.

ٱلْقَهَّار

Al-Qahhar

**The All-Subduer / The Irresistible**. Allah is the ultimately Dominant One, whose power and might subdue everything. All creation is under His command, and none can resist His will.

17.

ٱلْوَهَّاب

Al-Wahhab

**The All-Bountiful / The Bestower**. Allah is the Giver of Gifts, who grants favors and blessings freely, without expecting anything in return. His generosity is limitless and unconditional.

18.

ٱلرَّزَّاق

Ar-Razzaq

**The All-Provider / The Sustainer**. Allah is the ultimate Sustainer, who provides all forms of sustenance and livelihood to His creation, whether physical, spiritual, or emotional.

19.

ٱلْفَتَّاح

Al-Fattah

**The Opener / The Judge**. Allah is the Opener of all things, who opens the ways to success, victory, and knowledge. He also justly judges between His servants.

20.

ٱلْعَلِيم

Al-Alim

**The All-Knowing / The Omniscient**. Allah's knowledge encompasses everything: the seen and unseen, the past, present, and future, the apparent and hidden. Nothing is beyond His perfect knowledge.

21.

ٱلْقَابِض

Al-Qabid

**The Restrainer / The Withholder**. Allah is the one who withholds and restricts sustenance, blessings, and lives by His perfect wisdom. This manifests in periods of difficulty or scarcity.

22.

ٱلْبَاسِط

Al-Basit

**The Extender / The Giver of Abundance**. Allah is the one who expands and extends sustenance and blessings, bringing ease and prosperity. This is often mentioned in conjunction with Al-Qabid, showing His balanced control.

23.

ٱلْخَافِض

Al-Khafid

**The Abaser / The Humbler**. Allah is the One who lowers and abases the proud, the arrogant, and those who rebel against Him. He can humble anyone He wills.

24.

ٱلرَّافِع

Ar-Rafi'

**The Exalter / The Uplifter**. Allah is the One who raises the ranks of believers, exalts nations, and elevates anything He wills. He can uplift those who are humble and righteous.

25.

ٱلْمُعِزّ

Al-Mu'izz

**The Giver of Honor / The Honorer**. Allah is the one who grants honor, dignity, and power to whom He wills. True honor comes only from Him and is bestowed upon those He chooses.

26.

ٱلْمُذِلّ

Al-Muzill

**The Giver of Dishonor / The Humiliator**. Allah is the One who brings low and humiliates whom He wills. This is often a consequence for those who are arrogant or oppressive.

27.

ٱلسَّمِيع

As-Sami'

**The All-Hearing / The Hearer**. Allah hears every sound, whether loud or silent, apparent or hidden. His hearing encompasses all things, from the rustling of a leaf to the deepest whispers of the heart.

28.

ٱلْبَصِير

Al-Basir

**The All-Seeing / The Seer**. Allah sees all things, visible and invisible, minute or vast. Nothing is hidden from His sight, and He perceives the innermost thoughts and intentions.

29.

ٱلْحَكَم

Al-Hakam

**The Judge / The Arbitrator**. Allah is the ultimate Judge who decides justly between His creation. His judgments are always fair and righteous, and He is the ultimate authority in all disputes.

30.

ٱلْعَدْل

Al-Adl

**The Most Just / The Equitable**. Allah is perfectly just and equitable in all His actions and decrees. He does not oppress anyone, and His justice is absolute and without any bias.

31.

ٱلْلَّطِيف

Al-Latif

**The Subtle One / The Most Kind / The Gentle**. Allah is exceedingly subtle, gentle, and kind. He knows the most intricate and hidden things, and His kindness reaches His servants in gentle and imperceptible ways.

32.

ٱلْخَبِير

Al-Khabir

**The All-Aware / The Acquainted**. Allah is fully aware of everything, including the inner dimensions, hidden intentions, and every detail of His creation. Nothing can escape His comprehensive awareness.

33.

ٱلْحَلِيم

Al-Halim

**The Forbearing / The Most Clement**. Allah is incredibly forbearing and patient. He delays punishment for sins, allowing time for repentance and does not hasten His wrath, despite His immense power.

34.

ٱلْعَظِيم

Al-Azim

**The Magnificent / The Great One**. Allah is the Most Grand and Glorious, whose greatness is beyond human comprehension. His attributes, power, and glory are immense and majestic.

35.

ٱلْغَفُور

Al-Ghafur

**The All-Forgiving / The Exceedingly Forgiving**. This name emphasizes Allah's abundant and continuous forgiveness. He is the one who forgives sins repeatedly and covers up the faults of His servants.

36.

ٱلشَّكُور

Ash-Shakur

**The Most Appreciative / The Acknowledging of Thanks**. Allah is the one who appreciates and generously rewards even the smallest good deeds and sincere acts of worship. He multiplies rewards for His servants' efforts.

37.

ٱلْعَلِىّ

Al-Ali

**The Most High / The Most Exalted**. Allah is supremely high and exalted above all creation in His essence, attributes, and power. Nothing is above Him, and He is beyond all human comprehension.

38.

ٱلْكَبِير

Al-Kabir

**The Most Great / The Grand**. This name signifies Allah's absolute greatness and vastness. He is supreme in His attributes and unique in His power, making Him the greatest of all.

39.

ٱلْحَفِيظ

Al-Hafiz

**The Preserver / The Protector**. Allah is the ultimate Guardian and Preserver who protects and maintains all things, from the vast universe to the smallest atom. He safeguards creation from harm.

40.

ٱلْمُقِيت

Al-Muqit

**The Provider / The Maintainer / The Sustainer**. Allah is the one who sustains all creation, providing nourishment and power to all beings according to their needs. He has full control over sustenance.

41.

ٱلْحَسِيب

Al-Hasib

**The Accounter / The Reckoner / The Sufficient One**. Allah is the one who takes account of all deeds and provides sufficiency for His servants. He is sufficient for all needs and will hold everyone accountable.

42.

ٱلْجَلِيل

Al-Jalil

**The Majestic / The Sublime**. Allah is the embodiment of greatness, majesty, and glory. His attributes are magnificent, and He is revered and exalted above all.

43.

ٱلْكَرِيم

Al-Karim

**The Most Generous / The Bountiful / The Noble**. Allah is supremely generous, providing His blessings abundantly and unconditionally. He is noble, honorable, and forgives readily.

44.

ٱلرَّقِيب

Ar-Raqib

**The Ever-Watchful / The Watcher**. Allah constantly watches over all His creation. Nothing escapes His notice, and He is fully aware of every action, thought, and condition of His servants.

45.

ٱلْمُجِيب

Al-Mujib

**The Responder to Prayer / The Answerer**. Allah is the one who responds to the prayers and supplications of those who call upon Him. He answers according to His infinite wisdom and knowledge.

46.

ٱلْوَاسِع

Al-Wasi'

**The All-Encompassing / The Boundless**. Allah's knowledge, mercy, power, and bounty are vast and limitless. His embrace extends to all things, and His blessings are without bounds.

47.

ٱلْحَكِيم

Al-Hakim

**The All-Wise / The Wise**. Allah is perfect in His wisdom, knowledge, and judgment. Every action and decree from Him is based on ultimate wisdom and serves a divine purpose.

48.

ٱلْوَدُود

Al-Wadud

**The Most Loving / The Loving One**. Allah is the source of all love and shows immense love and affection towards His righteous servants. He loves those who do good and are repentant.

49.

ٱلْمَجِيد

Al-Majid

**The Most Glorious / The Illustrious**. Allah is the Glorious, whose glory is immense, magnificent, and whose qualities are noble. His majesty is profound and awe-inspiring.

50.

ٱلْبَاعِث

Al-Ba'ith

**The Resurrector / The Awakener**. Allah is the one who sends forth His messengers and resurrects the dead from their graves for accountability on the Day of Judgment, signifying His absolute power over life and death.

51.

ٱلشَّهِيد

Ash-Shahid

**The All-Witnessing**. The Witness, who is present and witnesses all things.

52.

ٱلْحَقّ

Al-Haqq

**The Absolute Truth**. The Truth, the Real, whose existence is undeniable.

53.

ٱلْوَكِيل

Al-Wakil

**The Disposer of Affairs / The Trustee**. The Guardian, on whom one can rely, who manages all affairs.

54.

ٱلْقَوِىّ

Al-Qawi

**The All-Strong**. The Powerful, whose strength is infinite.

55.

ٱلْمَتِين

Al-Matin

**The Firm / The Steadfast**. The Invincible, whose power is absolute and firm.

56.

ٱلْوَلِىّ

Al-Wali

**The Protecting Friend / The Patron**. The Supporter, the Protector, who is the closest ally to the believers.

57.

ٱلْحَمِيد

Al-Hamid

**The All-Praiseworthy**. The Praised, the Laudable, who is worthy of all praise.

58.

ٱلْمُحْصِى

Al-Muhsi

**The Accounter / The Reckoner**. The Enumerator, who comprehends and counts all things.

59.

ٱلْمُبْدِئ

Al-Mubdi'

**The Originator / The Producer**. The Initiator, who originates all creation.

60.

ٱلْمُعِيد

Al-Mu'id

**The Restorer / The Reproducer**. The Reproducer, who brings creation back after death.

61.

ٱلْمُحْيِى

Al-Muhyi

**The Giver of Life**. The Bestower of Life, who gives life to all things.

62.

ٱلْمُمِيت

Al-Mumit

**The Taker of Life / The Destroyer**. The Bringer of Death, who brings death to all living things.

63.

ٱلْحَىّ

Al-Hayy

**The Ever-Living**. The Living, who is eternal and never dies.

64.

ٱلْقَيُّوم

Al-Qayyum

**The Self-Subsisting / The Maintainer of All**. The Sustainer of all existence, who upholds all things.

65.

ٱلْوَاجِد

Al-Wajid

**The Perceiver / The Finder**. The All-Perceiving, who finds and achieves all He wills.

66.

ٱلْمَاجِد

Al-Majid

**The Illustrious / The Noble**. The Glorious, who is exalted in honor and abundant in goodness.

67.

ٱلْوَاحِد

Al-Wahid

**The Unique / The One**. The One, the Indivisible, who has no partners or equals.

68.

ٱلْأَحَد

Al-Ahad

**The Only One / The One and Only**. The Unique, the Absolute One, distinct from all creation.

69.

ٱلصَّمَد

As-Samad

**The Eternal / The Independent**. The Self-Sufficient Master, upon whom all depend, but He depends on none.

70.

ٱلْقَادِر

Al-Qadir

**The All-Powerful / The Able**. The Capable, who has the power to do all things.

71.

ٱلْمُقْتَدِر

Al-Muqtadir

**The All-Determining / The Irresistible Power**. The Omnipotent, who has supreme power and capability.

72.

ٱلْمُقَدِّم

Al-Muqaddim

**The Expediter / The Advancer**. The Foremost, who puts things in their proper order, advancing what He wills.

73.

ٱلْمُؤَخِّر

Al-Mu'akhkhir

**The Delayer / The Postponer**. The Postponer, who delays what He wills.

74.

ٱلْأَوَّل

Al-Awwal

**The First**. The First, who has no beginning.

75.

ٱلْآخِر

Al-Akhir

**The Last**. The Last, who has no end.

76.

ٱلظَّاهِر

Az-Zahir

**The Manifest / The Evident**. The Outward, whose existence is clear and evident.

77.

ٱلْبَاطِن

Al-Batin

**The Hidden / The Inner**. The Inward, who is hidden from creation's sight.

78.

ٱلْوَالِي

Al-Wali

**The Governor / The Ruler**. The Sovereign, who administers and governs all creation.

79.

ٱلْمُتَعَالِي

Al-Muta'ali

**The Most Exalted / The Supreme**. The Most High, who is above all human attributes.

80.

ٱلْبَرّ

Al-Barr

**The Source of All Goodness / The Righteous**. The Benefactor, the Good, who is the source of all righteousness and goodness.

81.

ٱلتَّوَّاب

At-Tawwab

**The Acceptor of Repentance**. The Relenting, who accepts repentance and pardons sins.

82.

ٱلْمُنْتَقِم

Al-Muntaqim

**The Avenger**. The Avenger, who punishes wrongdoers.

83.

ٱلْعَفُوّ

Al-Afu

**The Pardoner / The Eraser of Sins**. The Forgiver, who wipes out sins and grants pardon.

84.

ٱلرَّءُوف

Ar-Ra'uf

**The Most Kind / The Compassionate**. The Compassionate, who is full of tenderness and mercy.

85.

مَالِكُ ٱلْمُلْك

Malik-ul-Mulk

**The Master of All Sovereignty / King of all Kingdoms**. The Owner of Absolute Sovereignty, who has ultimate dominion over all.

86.

ذُو ٱلْجَلَالِ وَٱلْإِكْرَام

Dhul-Jalali Wal-Ikram

**The Possessor of Majesty and Honor**. The Lord of Majesty and Bounty, who is majestic and honorable.

87.

ٱلْمُقْسِط

Al-Muqsit

**The Just / The Equitable**. The Equitable, who administers justice with fairness.

88.

ٱلْجَامِع

Al-Jami'

**The Gatherer / The Collector**. The Gatherer, who gathers all creation on the Day of Judgment.

89.

ٱلْغَنِيّ

Al-Ghani

**The Self-Sufficient / The Rich**. The Independent, who is free of all needs and possess all wealth.

90.

ٱلْمُغْنِي

Al-Mughni

**The Enricher / The Sufficer**. The Enricher, who grants sufficiency and richness to whom He wills.

91.

ٱلْمَانِع

Al-Mani'

**The Preventer / The Withholder**. The Withholder, who prevents or withholds what He wills.

92.

ٱلضَّارّ

Ad-Darr

**The Distressor / The Harmer**. The Afflictor, who sends affliction to whom He wills.

93.

ٱلنَّافِع

An-Nafi'

**The Bestower of Benefits / The Propitious**. The Benefactor, who grants benefits to whom He wills.

94.

ٱلنُّور

An-Nur

**The Light**. The Light, who illuminates the heavens and the earth.

95.

ٱلْهَادِي

Al-Hadi

**The Guide**. The Guide, who guides His creation to the truth.

96.

ٱلْبَدِيع

Al-Badi'

**The Incomparable / The Originator**. The Originator, who creates in a wonderful and unparalleled way.

97.

ٱلْبَاقِي

Al-Baqi

**The Ever-Enduring / The Everlasting**. The Enduring, who remains forever and does not perish.

98.

ٱلْوَارِث

Al-Warith

**The Inheritor of All**. The Inheritor, who remains after all creation perishes.

99.

ٱلرَّشِيد

Ar-Rashid

**The Guide to the Right Path / The Righteous Teacher**. The Guide, who guides to righteousness and truth.

This page compiles information on the 99 Names of Allah (Asmaul Husna) based on Islamic traditions.
